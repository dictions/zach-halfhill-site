Zach Halfhill is a multidisciplinary artist from the midwest. He concentrates primarily on graphic design, art direction, branding, and illustration, but also explores set design, film photography, and street art. He currently works as a designer for Journeys and freelances in Nashville, Tennessee. Inspired by destruction and the disruption of comfort, his work is punk in ethos and reflects his daily obsession with the macabre. He is now accepting clients.
